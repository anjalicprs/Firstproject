Our Partners

With the abundance of floating talent and a plethora of ideas waiting to be tapped , it was time NIT Trichy created a conduit for students to expose themselves to the world of entrepreneurship. Thus came into being the Entrepreneurship Cell of NIT Trichy in the year 2005. With the spirit of enterprise being our motto , it’s been an eventful 8 years since our infancy, and we have been actively playing our part in the development of entrepreneurial ecosystem associated with youth and the student community globally. With a variety of events and activities under our belt, we provide ample opportunities for both the members and the non members of our E-Cell to develop their potential. If there’s a message from this , it’s all about culture. It took us quite a few years and a lot of hard work , but we have created a culture of entrepreneurship here and we aspire to carry it forward

E-Cell,Nit Trichy

  • To motivate and inspire students to take up the challenge of entrepreneurship
  • To equip them with necessary skills and provide all possible assistance.
  • Promote Creative thinking and an entrepreneurial mindset among the students.
  • Promote innovations and help convert them into market accepted products.
Our Vision
We look forward to a day when the first choice of students of the NIT Trichy and other institutes would be to start up on their own and Entrepreneurship-Cell , NIT Trichy will be there in every step of this journey of guidance and growth.
